## High Confidence Sources

### TM 9-2320-280-10

- Title: Operator's Manual for Truck, Utility: 1 1/4-Ton, 4x4, M998 Series.
- Edition referenced in research notes: 31 January 1996 with later changes.
- Use for: operator controls, startup flow, PMCS checks, instrument/gauge behavior,
  transfer-case operating guidance, HMMWV performance text.
- Confirmed values:
  - Fully loaded HMMWV grade capability: 60% / 31 degrees.
  - Side-slope capability: 40% / 22 degrees.
  - Trailer towing note: maximum safe side slope reduced from 40% to 30%.
  - Oil pressure below 6 psi at idle is a no-go condition.
  - Coolant above 230 F / 110 C is a no-go condition.
  - Air restriction indicator red is a no-go condition.
- Caveat: research found these grade/side-slope values as text, not as an
  illustrated HMMWV slope diagram in this TM.

### TC 21-305-20 / AFMAN 24-306(I)

- Edition referenced: 12 January 2016.
- Use for: illustrated HMMWV slope/climb capability reference.
- Known figure: Figure 7-13, "HMMWV Slope and Climb Capabilities", page 7-11.
- Caveat: use the figure for visual inspiration only after verifying the page image.

### TC 21-305-4

- Title: Training Program for the High Mobility Multipurpose Wheeled Vehicle.
- Edition referenced: 31 May 1991.
- Use for: HMMWV lesson-style driving technique, especially off-road/desert training.
- Caveat: no verified HMMWV slope/climb diagram found in the searched copy.

### FM 21-305

- Title: Manual for the Wheeled Vehicle Driver.
- Edition referenced: 27 August 1993.
- Use for: general wheeled-vehicle driving technique.
- Caveat: vehicle-agnostic; do not cite for HMMWV-specific slope diagram.

### FM 90-3

- Title: Desert Operations.
- Edition referenced: 24 August 1993.
- Use for: desert trafficability and dune/slope technique context.
- Caveat: generic terrain guidance, not HMMWV-specific capability values.

### ATPD-2099E

- Use for: HMMWV vertical step test context.
- Game use: Stage 3 obstacle quiz currently uses an 18 inch vertical step threshold.
- Caveat: variant/equipment details matter; keep this as a training threshold, not
  an exact universal field limit.

### FM 5-102

- Use for: obstacle and log-obstacle engineering context.
- Game use: Stage 3 tree/log obstacle threshold is simplified and source-caveated.

### Wikipedia

- Pages: English and Hebrew Humvee pages.
- Use for: Stage 10 trivia only: name, manufacturer, service history, basic specs,
  replacement-program context.
- Caveat: do not use Wikipedia for operator limits or safety-critical behavior.

# Stage Mapping

Tags: #stages #prd #gameplay

## Current Stage Order

1. Driver Controls - control hotspots and startup sequence.
2. Transmission & T-Case - focused shifter layout and range-change lesson.
3. Obstacle Judgment - randomized passable/impassable terrain quiz.
4. City Driving - curved roads, traffic control, reverse, off-road failure.
5. Off-Road & Gears - applied gear/range gates on terrain.
6. Technical Off-Road - grade, descent, rear/isometric side-slope, traction, fording.
7. Trailer Parking - articulated M149-style trailer reversing.
8. Pre/Post Checks - tire, oil, fluids, belts, air restriction, leaks, hubs.
9. Gauge Scan - quiz on oil, coolant temp, fuel, speed/odometer, voltmeter.
10. Humvee Trivia - Wikipedia-based non-operational trivia.

## Design Decision

Teach concepts before applying them:

- Stage 2 teaches the levers without movement.
- Stage 3 teaches terrain judgment without vehicle-control pressure.
- Stages 4-7 apply those concepts while driving.
